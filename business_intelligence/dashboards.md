## 1. Executive Summary Dashboard
A high-level overview for top management.

### Key Components:
- **KPI Cards**
  - Total Revenue (MTD/YTD)
  - Total Units Sold
  - Active Alerts Count
  - Forecast Accuracy %
- **Trend Line**
  - Monthly sales revenue (12-month view)
- **Top 5 Products**
  - Ranked by revenue

### Purpose:
Provide instant visibility into business health.

---

## 2. Audit Dashboard
Focused on employee actions, policy violations, and system restrictions.

### Key Components:
- **Violation Count (Daily/Weekly)**
- **Actions Blocked vs Allowed**
- **Top Users Triggering Violations**
- **List of Holiday/Weekday Violations**
- **Activity Timeline**
  - Insert/update/delete attempts

### Purpose:
Ensure compliance with business rules & provide operational transparency.

---

## 3. Inventory & Performance Dashboard
Built for inventory and operations managers.

### Key Components:
- **Stockout Risk Heatmap**
- **Products Below Safety Stock**
- **Inventory Movement Logs**
  - Receipts, Returns, Adjustments
- **Forecast-Based Replenishment Recommendations**

### Purpose:
Support timely inventory decisions and reduce stockout events.

---

## 4. Customer Insights Dashboard
Designed for marketing and customer engagement.

### Key Components:
- Customer segments (pie chart)
- Average segment score
- Repeat customers vs one-time buyers
- Top 10 customers by revenue
- City/Country sales distribution

### Purpose:
Enable targeted marketing & customer behavior analysis.
